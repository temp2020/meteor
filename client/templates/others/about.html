Vastershops is an application that offers users to sell their products and services anywhere in the world and ideally around them. Users can post their requests for purchases of products, services or rent the services of a third party, hire and seek...  Vastershops app has the most modern tools of digital communication and includes a search engine that locates, for buyers, products and services thanks to the GPS system present on smartphones. Buyers know where the product / service is offered.
Professionals and those who wish to do it can create their own Store with their brand and sell as many products as they want. It takes less than 10 seconds to create your shop. Go into profile, enter a name for your sign, choose an image in the gallery. You are done! Your shop is created! Buyers can sent their requests to the market. 
The application integrates a system of chat and messaging. Buyers can rate sellers after a certain number of transactions.

Vastershops is primarly a B2B and B2C/C2B market for professionnals.
